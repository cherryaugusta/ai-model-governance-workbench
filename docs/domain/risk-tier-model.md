# Risk Tier Model

Risk tier determines how strict release governance must be.

## Supported tiers

- low
- medium
- high
- critical

## Why risk tier exists

Different systems should not follow identical approval expectations. A customer-facing or sensitive workflow needs stricter review than a lower-risk internal helper.

## Governance impact

Risk tier influences:
- required approval types
- operational scrutiny
- release interpretation
- portfolio realism

## Design posture

The model is intentionally simple for v1, but still meaningful enough to justify policy-driven approval behavior.
